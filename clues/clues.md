## The 1947 Massacre

### old-village-was-lemko
[%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) was a Lemko settlement — Greek Catholic, with a cerkiew, orchards, and a tightly-knit community.

### massacre-happened
In 1947, soldiers came to deport the Lemkos from [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md). The villagers resisted. The soldiers opened fire. The whole rebelling population was killed.

### massacre-was-covered-up
The massacre was never reported. The participating soldiers made a pact of silence. The state erased it from records.

### soldier-participated-in-massacre
[%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md) was one of the soldiers present during the 1947 massacre. He participated in the killings.

### soldier-took-best-land
[%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md) settled in [%NEW_VILLAGE%](../places/new-village.md) early because he knew the terrain from service. He claimed the best plot of land — profiting from the destruction.

### old-village-was-burned
[%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) was burned after the massacre. Wooden buildings destroyed, but stone survived — the cerkiew, foundations, the well.

### massacre-bodies-in-well
The remains of the 1947 massacre victims are in [the well](../story-facts/the-well.md). They were never properly buried.

### dead-never-mourned
The 1947 dead were never given proper rites — no panakhyda, no memorial, no mourning. Twenty years of spiritual debt.

### dam-covers-evidence
The flood zone was chosen partly to bury the massacre evidence. [%OFFICER%](../characters/officer.md) knows this. The dam is infrastructure *and* a burial.

### hag-caused-the-massacre
[%HAG%](../characters/hag.md) was in love with a UPA soldier in 1947. The authorities traced the connection back to the village. The massacre happened because of her.

## The 1954 Lynch

### matrona-caught-lovers
[%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md) caught [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md) and [%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) together.

### matrona-orchestrated-lynch
[%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md) orchestrated the drinking that led to the lynch. She stayed sober, aimed her brothers at [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md), and said "get rid of the problem." She never touched anyone — every hand that night was male.

### three-drunk-attackers
[%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md), [%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md), and [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) were the three drunk men who attacked [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md).

### jagna-was-raped
[%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md) was beaten and raped by [%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) and [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md). She died from the violence.

### soldier-killed-defending-daughter
[%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md) tried to save [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md) and was killed.

### soldiers-wife-murdered
[%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md)'s wife was murdered to eliminate witnesses.

### glupek-strangled
[%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) tried to strangle [%GLUPEK%](../characters/glupek.md) as an infant. [%CIOTKA%](../characters/ciotka.md) stopped him. The oxygen deprivation caused permanent brain damage — it was not congenital.

### lynch-bodies-in-well
The bodies from the 1954 lynch were dumped in the old well in [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) — the same well that already held the 1947 massacre remains.

### wujas-loved-jagna
[%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) was in love with [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md), but she chose [%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md).

### painter-was-spared
[%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) was beaten but spared because he was [%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md)'s fiancé.

### painter-heard-matrona
[%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) heard [%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md)'s voice giving instructions during the lynch.

### ciotka-saved-glupek
[%CIOTKA%](../characters/ciotka.md) physically intervened to save [%GLUPEK%](../characters/glupek.md)'s life that night. The family has never forgiven her.

### priest-knows-everything
[%PRIEST%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) knows the full lynch story — who did what, where the bodies went — because [%CIOTKA%](../characters/ciotka.md) confessed it all to him over 13 years.

### neighbour-heard-the-lynch
[%NEIGHBOUR%](../characters/neighbour.md) heard everything from next door — screaming, shouting, dogs. Possibly saw men dragging bodies toward [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md). Closed the curtain and never spoke.

## The Well

### well-holds-two-sets-of-dead
The well holds two sets of dead — the 1947 massacre victims and the 1954 lynch victims.

### butcher-visits-the-well
[%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) visits [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) regularly. Sits near the well. Clears debris. His dogs refuse to approach.

### butcher-compelled-to-feed
[%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) feels compelled to put something IN the well. Strangers = prey. He doesn't fully understand the compulsion.

### hag-tends-the-well
[%HAG%](../characters/hag.md) has spent 20 years performing Lemko rites at the well — fire, incense, bread, honey, prayer. She is containing it through acknowledgement.

### butcher-vs-hag
Someone keeps clearing debris from the well. Someone else keeps piling it back and leaving candle wax in the cerkiew. [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) and [%HAG%](../characters/hag.md) are fighting over the well without knowing each other.

### dogs-wont-cross
[%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md)'s dogs stop at a perimeter around the well and refuse to cross it. He crosses every time.

### players-dream-too
From the first night in the village, the players have dreams of violence — hands doing things, the satisfaction of breaking. Each night worse. By day three they've felt what [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md) has felt for thirteen years.

### babcia-mind-returns
[%BABCIA%](../characters/babcia.md)'s dementia reverses as the well strengthens. Nobody notices because nobody was watching.

### babcia-hears-singing
[%BABCIA%](../characters/babcia.md) has heard [%HAG%](../characters/hag.md)'s Lemko prayers carried on the wind at night. She will notice when the singing stops.

### glupek-drawn-to-well
[%GLUPEK%](../characters/glupek.md) is physically drawn toward [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) and the well. At the well he becomes still — something in him recognises the place.

### covered-mirrors
[%BABCIA%](../characters/babcia.md) covers every mirror in [%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md)'s house. In [Lemko tradition](../historical%20context/13-lemko-beliefs-and-folk-magic.md), mirrors are covered when someone dies. She's been doing it for twenty years.

## Village Life & Relationships

### barbara-knows-nothing
[%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md) arrived in [%NEW_VILLAGE%](../places/new-village.md) after the lynch. She knows nothing about the massacre, [%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md)'s family, or [the well](../story-facts/the-well.md).

### paweleks-father
[%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) is [%PAWEŁEK%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s actual father. Nobody knows — not even [%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md). [%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) wrongly believes [%JUNIOR%](../characters/junior.md) is the father.

### wojewoda-built-barbaras-house
[%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) built [%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md) a house out of guilt — he suspects [%JUNIOR%](../characters/junior.md) fathered [%PAWEŁEK%](../characters/secondary-characters.md). He's wrong.

### barbara-is-a-sieve
[%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md) shares what she hears from the committee casually with [%NEIGHBOUR%](../characters/neighbour.md) over the fence. Everything the players tell her reaches the one man sitting on 13 years of guilt.

### wife-has-been-investigating
[%WIFE%](../characters/wife.md) has spent years quietly investigating the events of 1954. She and [%JUNIOR%](../characters/junior.md) work together in secret.

### flour-tin-evidence
[%WIFE%](../characters/wife.md) keeps her evidence in a flour tin in the pantry — a stained shirt, a mismatched button, newspaper clippings, handwritten notes.

### junior-is-false-suspect
[%JUNIOR%](../characters/junior.md) looks like the problem — violent record, sneaking around at night — but he's actually helping [%WIFE%](../characters/wife.md) investigate the truth. His brawl record is real but unrelated.

### wujas-is-the-weak-link
[%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) is the weakest link in the lynch cover-up. He's been drinking to forget for 13 years. Has let fragments slip: "She didn't deserve it." The name %JAGNA%. "Don't ever be like us."

### matrona-controls-painter
[%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md) keeps [%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) broken, dependent, and terrified. He knows the truth. She's made sure he'll never speak it.

### painter-wants-to-confess
[%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) secretly wants [%JAGNA%](../characters/jagna.md) included in the census so she is not erased by the flood.

### ciotka-lives-in-soldiers-house
[%CIOTKA%](../characters/ciotka.md) lives in [%SOLDIER%](../characters/soldier.md)'s former house — the best plot in the village — raising his brain-damaged son. A single woman in a dead man's house with no clean explanation.

### glupek-fears-butcher
[%GLUPEK%](../characters/glupek.md) flinches instinctively from [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md). No conscious memory — just a body that knows. When [%BUTCHER%](../characters/butcher.md)'s dogs bark, [%GLUPEK%](../characters/glupek.md) hides.

### wojewoda-has-only-phone
[%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) has the only telephone in the village, in his office. The village's sole link to the outside world.

### wojewoda-has-gun
[%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) has a pistol in his office. Locked. Symbol of authority — but loaded.

### neighbour-has-rifle
[%NEIGHBOUR%](../characters/neighbour.md) is a hunter with a licensed hunting rifle.

### siblings-fund-the-church
The siblings keep the [church](../places/new-village.md#church) unusually well supplied — firewood, food, gifts. [%PRIEST%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s silence is materially supported.

### painters-art-shows-the-well
[%PAINTER%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s art starts showing the well before the players find it — dark circles, stone rings, black water.

## The Flood & The Committee

### new-village-may-flood
The committee discovers that [%NEW_VILLAGE%](../places/new-village.md) may also be in the flood zone. The geologist needs field measurements to confirm.

### wojewoda-already-suspects-flooding
[%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) already suspects the flood is coming before the committee arrives. He sees it as an opportunity — water swallows the evidence.

### road-washes-out
Day 2 brings a major flood. The road out disappears. The committee is stranded. No outside backup.

### officer-warning
[%OFFICER%](../characters/officer.md) warns on the drive in: not everything needs to be written down.

### phone-is-lifeline
The players can call [Professor](../characters/secondary-characters.md) by phone. If they tell him about the massacre, the truth exists outside the village.

## The Ritual

### ritual-requires-three-things
The ritual requires form (how), words (what to say), and truth (naming the dead and what happened).

### hag-has-the-form
[%HAG%](../characters/hag.md)'s cabin contains icons, candles, herbs, prayer materials — the ritual's form. If alive, she can teach it directly.

### babcia-has-the-words
[%BABCIA%](../characters/babcia.md) can provide the panakhyda — the Lemko memorial prayers for the dead.

### players-supply-truth
The players must supply the truth by naming the dead and saying what happened to them. The ritual is not a spell — it is acknowledgement.

### barbara-must-bring-babcia
[%BARBARA%](../characters/barbara.md) is the only one who can physically bring [%BABCIA%](../characters/babcia.md) to the well. She won't do it without [%PRIEST%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s explicit blessing.

## Red Herrings & Side Mysteries

### foreman-embezzlement
[%FOREMAN%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) is stealing from the PGR farm — phantom workers, skimmed supplies, padded expenses. [%ZOFIA%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) helps. Looks suspicious, unrelated to the murders.

### bimber-still
[%WUJAS%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s crew runs an illegal bimber still in the [forest](../places/forest.md), halfway between the villages. Just moonshine — not the murder conspiracy. [%WOJEWODA%](../characters/wojewoda.md) knows and tolerates it. [%MATRONA%](../characters/matrona.md) supplies sugar through the store.

### paweleks-illness
[%PAWEŁEK%](../characters/secondary-characters.md) drinks contaminated floodwater near the well and falls ill. Medically ordinary for flood conditions — but his fever dreams mention "the water under the ground" and whispers near the well.

### old-village-looks-haunted
[%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) looks haunted — candlelight, incense, footprints — because [%HAG%](../characters/hag.md) is tending it, not because it's empty.

### cemetery-missing-graves
The cemetery at [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) has fewer graves than it should. Some dead are missing because they are in the well.

### upa-bunker
The UPA bunker in the [forest](../places/forest.md) contains old partisan ordnance. Partially collapsed, dangerous. [%FOREMAN%](../characters/secondary-characters.md)'s flood-diversion plan uses explosives from here.
