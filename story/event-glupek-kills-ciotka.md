# %GLUPEK% Kills %CIOTKA%

**Type:** Story event — triggered / variable timing

## Trigger

%GLUPEK% learns the truth about his parents — that they were murdered, that the siblings were responsible, that %CIOTKA% was there that night. How he learns is variable:
- The players tell him (deliberately or carelessly)
- He overhears a confrontation between the players and the villagers
- %WUJAS% lets it slip while drunk
- A crisis around the well forces the truth into the open

This event **only fires if %GLUPEK% learns the truth**. If the players are careful with information, it may never happen.

## What Happens

%GLUPEK% confronts %CIOTKA%. Not with words — he can barely speak. With anguish. Confusion. Rage he doesn't understand and can't control. He grabs her the way %BUTCHER% once grabbed him — by the throat. He doesn't intend to kill her. He's 17, massive, brain-damaged, and his whole world just collapsed.

**He strangles her.** The woman who saved his life, who raised him, who loved him for 13 years. Dead by the same method that damaged him as a child. The cycle completes.

## Aftermath

%GLUPEK% runs. The village thinks he's fled into the forest or the ruins. **In reality, he's hiding in %PRIEST%'s church.** The one other safe place he's ever known — the man who protected him alongside %CIOTKA%.

%PRIEST% may or may not know %GLUPEK% is there. If he does, he's sheltering a killer — the brain-damaged boy he swore to protect, now covered in the blood of the woman they both loved.

## What This Does to the Village

- **The silence shatters.** %CIOTKA%'s death forces the truth to the surface. The village can no longer pretend.
- **%BUTCHER% sees his unfinished business.** The boy he tried to kill 13 years ago just proved he was right — the child should have died that night. Does he go hunting?
- **%WOJEWODA% loses control.** His sister is dead, killed by the boy everyone knows is connected to the family his brothers destroyed. The cover-up is collapsing.
- **The players face a choice.** Find %GLUPEK% before %BUTCHER% does? Protect him? Turn him in? To whom — there's no law here, the road is flooded.
- **%PRIEST% faces his dilemma.** Shelter the boy and become complicit, or give him up to a village that may kill him.

## Open Questions

- What day does this happen? (Variable — depends on when %GLUPEK% learns the truth)
- Can the players prevent it? (If they control the flow of information, yes)
- Does %CIOTKA% understand what's happening? Does she resist?
- Does %PRIEST% actively hide %GLUPEK%, or does the boy just appear in the church?
