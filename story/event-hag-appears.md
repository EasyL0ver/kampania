# Paraskewia Chyłak Appears at %OLD_VILLAGE%

**Type:** Story event — atmospheric / clue delivery

## Trigger

The intensifying rain drives Paraskewia Chyłak out of the forest and into the open. She believes the downpour is not natural — it's the **wrath of the unquiet dead**. The massacre victims, the lynch victims, all the unmourned souls in the well — they are furious, and the earth is answering.

Her 20 years of ritual were not enough. The dead want more than candles and bread. They want the truth.

## What Happens

Paraskewia Chyłak appears at the ruins of %OLD_VILLAGE% — not hiding this time. She's at the *cerkiew*, performing her rites openly, desperately. Louder. Fires burning despite the rain. Chanting in Lemko that carries on the wind.

The villagers who see her are terrified. The "ghost" of %OLD_VILLAGE% is real — a flesh-and-blood old woman, ancient, wild, performing pagan rites in a ruined church in the middle of a flood.

## What She Can Tell the Players

If the players reach her and can communicate (she speaks broken Polish, mostly Lemko):

- **The massacre.** She was there in 1947. She saw the soldiers. She saw the bodies.
- **The well.** She knows where the dead were put. She's been praying over it for 20 years.
- **The land's memory.** In her worldview, the rain is punishment — the dead were never given justice, never mourned properly, and now they're dragging everything down with them.

She doesn't know about the lynch — she doesn't know Edward Barnaś's family is in the well too. If the players tell her, it would devastate her. She's been praying for the Lemko dead. She didn't know there were more.

## What She Won't Do

- She won't come to %NEW_VILLAGE%. She hasn't set foot there in 20 years.
- She won't talk to Zbigniew Gajda or any villager. They are the people who built their homes on her people's graves.
- She won't leave the area. The water is rising and she knows it. She's not leaving. This is where she dies.

## Open Questions

- What day does she appear? (Variable — tied to rain intensity)
- Can the players bring her information that changes her mind about anything?
- Does she recognize the siblings' Lemko heritage? What would that mean to her?
- Does she interact with Edek Barnaś if he's nearby?
