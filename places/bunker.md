# The Bunker — UPA Ziemianka

**Type:** Location — hidden, forest

## Description

A forgotten UPA partisan bunker (*ziemianka*) buried deep in the forest between %NEW_VILLAGE% and %OLD_VILLAGE%. Built in the mid-1940s during the Ukrainian insurgency, it was one of many underground hideouts scattered across the Bieszczady. The army destroyed most of them after Akcja Wisła — this one was missed.

By 1967 it's been abandoned for 20 years. The entrance is nearly invisible — overgrown, hidden under moss and fallen trees. Inside: a low-ceilinged earth dugout reinforced with rotting timber, damp and dark. Narrow tunnels branch out, one leading toward the outskirts of %OLD_VILLAGE%.

## The Tunnels

The bunker has a tunnel network originally built for escape and covert movement. One tunnel emerges near the ruins of %OLD_VILLAGE% — close enough to the *cerkiew* and the cemetery.

**%HAG% uses this tunnel** to reach %OLD_VILLAGE% without being seen by the colonists. This is how she has been performing her rituals at the *cerkiew* for 20 years without anyone catching her — she goes underground, surfaces near the ruins, does what she needs to do, and vanishes. The villagers see ghost lights and hear singing, but never find footprints leading from %NEW_VILLAGE%.

## What the Players Might Find

- A collapsed section of tunnel exposed by the heavy rain — earth giving way, timber visible.
- Strange airflow from a hillside — ventilation shaft, still working after 20 years.
- %GLUPEK% sitting near the hidden entrance — does he know it's there? Does he go inside?
- Signs of recent use — worn path underground, candle wax, the smell of herbs.
- Old UPA artifacts — rusted weapons, rotting supplies, Cyrillic inscriptions carved into timber.

## Open Questions

- Where does %HAG% actually live? A separate shelter deeper in the forest? A cave? Another bunker?
- Do any villagers know the bunker exists? (Unlikely — it was built by UPA, not locals.)
- Could the rising water flood the tunnels, cutting off %HAG%'s access to %OLD_VILLAGE%?
- What else is down there? UPA left things behind — documents, weapons, bodies?
