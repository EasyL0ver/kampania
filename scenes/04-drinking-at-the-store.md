# The Store

**Location:** %MATRONA%'s general store — %HALINA% behind the counter, drinking crew outside on benches and crates
**Present:** %HALINA% (always inside), %WUJAS% (always outside), %NEIGHBOUR% (usually outside), other village men. %BUTCHER% is notably absent — doesn't drink.
**Available:** Daytime, any day. Repeatable.
**Cost:** 1 action per visit

## Setup

The village social hub. Outside — men on benches passing a bottle of bimber. %WUJAS% is always here. %NEIGHBOUR% drifts in and out. Lazy, circular, bitter talk — the state, the weather, the dam. Nobody talks about the past unless they slip.

Inside — %HALINA% runs the counter. %MATRONA% owns the place but rarely gets her hands dirty. Shelves sparse but functional. Bimber under the counter. The crew drifts in to buy rounds, banter with %HALINA%, get shooed out. She has a mean streak — sharp tongue, doesn't suffer fools. She resents working for %MATRONA% and doesn't hide it.

Government people get suspicion outside. But if the players have rapport with %WUJAS% — he waves them over, pours a drink. They're in.

## What Can Be Learned (drinking)

- **From %WUJAS%** — fragments about the past. Half-sentences he regrets. Gets darker the drunker he gets.
- **From %NEIGHBOUR%** — nervousness, bad lies, sounds and directions he can't keep in.
- **From the crew in general** — village gossip, social dynamics, who's been acting strange lately.

## Opportunities

- **Buy a bottle for the crew** — put money on the counter, tell %HALINA% it's for the boys outside. Word travels fast. → Rapport with drinking crew
- **Village gossip** — who's sleeping with whom, who owes what. → Bonus info: social map of the village
- **Learn about Wujas** — the crew talks about him like furniture. He's always here, always drunk, always tolerated. → Bonus info: %WUJAS%'s place in the village — the wreck everyone enables
- **Learn about the drinking crew** — who these men are, what they do, how they relate to each other. → Bonus info: crew dynamics and pecking order
- **Gossip about Foreman** — their boss at the PGR. The crew has opinions. → Bonus info: what %FOREMAN% is like from below — how he runs things, what the workers think of him
- **Junior joins** — if rapport with %JUNIOR% is high, he shows up. Drinks with the crew, loosens up away from his father. → Rapport with %JUNIOR% deepens + bonus info: he's different without %WOJEWODA% watching
- **Side with Halina against the drunks** — when the crew hassles her, back her up. → Rapport with %HALINA%
- **Halina complains about Matrona** — she doesn't need much prompting. Bad boss, tight with money, controls everything. → Bonus info: %MATRONA%'s grip on the village economy
- **Neighbour's nervousness** — he's jumpy, drinks too fast. If the past comes up, he goes quiet. → Bonus info: the past rattles him

## Actions

### Talk to Halina privately
- **Requires:** Rapport with %HALINA% or the crew is outside (not hovering)
- **Cost:** Free
- **Outcome:** She sees everyone who comes through. Knows village patterns — who buys what, who stopped coming in, who's been acting different. She's not a keeper of secrets, but she's observant and resentful enough to talk.
- **Leads to:** Bonus info on village patterns and NPC behaviour. If asked about %MATRONA% — she'll vent, may reveal how the store and village economy really work.
