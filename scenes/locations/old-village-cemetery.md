## Setup

Lemko cemetery. Cyrillic stone crosses and headstones. The dead who were buried properly — before 1947.

**Current state (1967):**
- Headstones tilted or toppled; inscriptions weathered
- Graves subsiding as wood coffins rot
- Ground soggy, overgrown with vegetation; difficult to walk through
- **Fewer graves than expected** for a village this size

## Opportunities

- **Cyrillic headstones** — confirm Lemko/Greek Catholic community. → Seeds [old-village-was-lemko](../../clues/clues.md#old-village-was-lemko)
- **The discrepancy** — visibly too few graves for a village population. Where are the rest of the dead? → Seeds [cemetery-missing-graves](../../clues/clues.md#cemetery-missing-graves)
- **Names and dates** — headstones give family names, stop abruptly at 1947. A village that simply ended.

## GM Notes

The cemetery's job is to make players count. The math doesn't work — not enough graves for the number of foundations visible. The missing dead are in the [well](the-well.md). Players who notice the discrepancy have the first hard evidence that something beyond a deportation happened here.
