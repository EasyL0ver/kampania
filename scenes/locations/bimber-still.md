# Bimber Still

**Type:** Location (discoverable)
**Location:** Hidden clearing off a forest path, roughly halfway between %NEW_VILLAGE% and %OLD_VILLAGE%. Near a stream.
**Present:** [Tadek Gajda](../../characters/wujas.md) and his drinking crew (Szymek Kępa, Romek Głowacz, Franek Mucha) — irregular, mostly evenings.
**Available:** Requires forest exploration or following Tadek/crew.
**Cost:** 1 action

## Setup

Crude copper still under camouflage netting. Surrounded by empty sugar sacks, barrels, firewood. Small fire pit with signs of regular use. Worn footpath leading to it — subtle but visible to a careful eye.

**Current state (1967):**
- Actively in use
- Smoke and fermentation smell detectable from nearby paths
- Sugar supplied by [Helena Rzepka](../../characters/matrona.md)'s [store](the-store.md); [Zbigniew Gajda](../../characters/wojewoda.md) knows and tolerates it

## Opportunities

- **The still and brewing equipment** — obviously illegal
- **Footprints and worn trail** from %NEW_VILLAGE%
- **Sugar sacks** traceable to [Helena Rzepka](../../characters/matrona.md)'s store
- **Empty bottles, drinking debris**
- **Tadek talking** — if players catch him drunk here, he's at his most vulnerable. The well makes the drinking stop numbing. Half-sentences may leak. → Seeds [wujas-is-the-weak-link](../../clues/clues.md#wujas-is-the-weak-link)

## GM Notes

**Red herring.** Looks like a major secret — hidden forest activity, nervous men, concealment. It's just bimber. See [bimber-operation.md](../../story-facts/bimber-operation.md) for full details.

**NOT near the well or [Paraskewia Chyłak](../../characters/hag.md)'s cabin** — but in the same forest. Players who "solve" the forest after finding the still may stop looking deeper. That's the trap.
