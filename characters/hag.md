# Paraskewia Chyłak

**Type:** Named character — Lemko woman, forest hermit

## Vital Statistics

- **Born:** ~1927
- **Age in 1947:** ~20
- **Age in 1967:** ~40
- **Heritage:** Lemko
- **Lives in:** [Hag's cabin](../scenes/locations/hags-cabin.md) — alone
- **Settled:** Never left — hid in the forest since the 1947 massacre

## Character

Lemko woman, 40, weathered by 20 years of forest living. Survived the 1947 massacre. Her forbidden love triggered the soldiers' arrival. Has been performing Lemko death rites at [the well](../story-facts/the-well.md) for 20 years — the well's keeper and containment.

## Relationships

- **[Stanisław Rezeń](butcher.md)** — doesn't know she exists. He's noticed signs: debris re-piled on the well after he cleared it, candle wax in the cerkiew, faint incense on nights nobody should be there. **She** knows exactly who he is — the local boy from the next valley who helped the soldiers in 1947. She's been watching his footprints at the well for 20 years.
