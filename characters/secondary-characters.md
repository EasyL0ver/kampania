## PAWEŁEK's Circle

### %STASZEK%
**Type:** Child — %PAWEŁEK%'s friend  
**Age in 1967:** 5  
**Parents:** %FOREMAN% and %ZOFIA%  
**Role:** A slightly older village child. Plays with %PAWEŁEK% before the illness. Innocent witness to where %PAWEŁEK% drank contaminated water (or claims not to remember). Could be questioned by the players or could provide partial information about children's activities in the flood.

**Notes:** May or may not fall ill himself after %PAWEŁEK%. His parents' reaction to his friend's sickness affects the village's fear level.

## %WUJAS%'s Drinking Circle

### %BUMP_ONE% / %SZYMEK%
**Type:** Village drunk — %WUJAS%'s regular drinking companion  
**Age in 1967:** ~45-55  
**Role:** A rough, crude man who drinks with %WUJAS% regularly. Knows the village's underbelly and rumors. Loose-tongued when drunk, but also mean-spirited—quick to laugh at others' misery. More likely to spread gossip than truth.

**Notes:** May have been present at or near the lynch, or may know fragments through boozy village talk. Unreliable but talkative.

### %BUMP_TWO% / %ROMEK%
**Type:** Village drunk — %WUJAS%'s regular drinking companion  
**Age in 1967:** ~40-50  
**Role:** A quieter drunk than %SZYMEK%, but observant. Listens more than talks. Has picked up details about the village's secrets through years of sitting in bars and watching. Less likely to volunteer information, but can be drawn out carefully. Knows %WUJAS% better than most—they have a deeper, more genuine friendship underneath the drinking.

**Notes:** Could be an ally if the players gain his trust. Might protect %WUJAS% or might eventually turn on him depending on circumstances.

### %BUMP_THREE% / %FRANEK%
**Type:** Village drunk — troublemaker  
**Age in 1967:** ~35-45  
**Role:** Younger and more aggressive than the other two. A brawler type, looking for fights when drunk. Drinks with %WUJAS% but there's tension—they have history, possibly a dispute over a woman or money. The most physically dangerous person at the store on any given day. Government people asking questions is exactly the kind of provocation that sets him off.

**Notes:** The wild card of the group. Unpredictable. Could provide information, or could become physically threatening. Respects strength and directness. If the players mishandle the drinking crew, Franek is the one who swings first.

## %MATRONA% and %PAINTER%'s Household

### %DAUGHTER% / %Ewa%
**Type:** Young woman — %MATRONA% and %PAINTER%'s eldest child  
**Age in 1967:** ~13-15  
**Role:** Worried about her father—she sees his fragility and broken state. This concern for %PAINTER% is her defining trait. Could be sympathetic to players or protective of her family depending on the situation. A potential crack in %MATRONA%'s control.

**Notes:** If players can reach %EWA%, she might reveal things about the household dynamic or her mother's cruelty.

### %KRYSTIAN%
**Type:** Child — %MATRONA% and %PAINTER%'s youngest, altar boy  
**Age in 1967:** ~8-9  
**Role:** Serves at the church assisting %PRIEST%. Sees and hears confessional traffic, notices who comes and goes. %MATRONA% placed him in this role to maintain the family's pious reputation. Innocent but observant. First loyalty is to %MATRONA%.

**Notes:** May witness %GLUPEK% hiding in the church. His cooperation with players depends on whether they can separate him from his mother's influence.

## %MATRONA%'s Store

### %SHOPKEEPER% / %Halina%
**Type:** Store worker — %MATRONA%'s employee  
**Age in 1967:** ~35-45  
**Role:** Works in %MATRONA%'s small general store. Deals with customers, manages stock, handles money. Sees everyone who comes through—knows the village's patterns and gossip. Resentful of working for %MATRONA% and struggles with the drinking crew (%SZYMEK%, %ROMEK%, %FRANEK%) who hang around and cause trouble in the store.

**Notes:** A gatekeeper to the store with a mean streak. Could be sympathetic to players if they take her side against the drunks, or hostile if they seem to align with her antagonists. Her resentment makes her more likely to complain about %MATRONA% than to protect her secrets.

## %WOJEWODA%'s Farm Workers

### %FOREMAN% / %Stefan%
**Type:** Farm overseer — promoted to primary character  
**See:** `characters\foreman.md`

### %WORKER_ONE% / %JOZEK%
**Type:** Farm laborer — migrant or day worker  
**Age in 1967:** ~30-40  
**Role:** A common farmhand, possibly migrant or semi-permanent. Does physical labor—plowing, hauling, repairs. Works alongside %BARBARA%. May be aware of her presence without necessarily knowing her secrets. Drinks with %WUJAS%'s circle or has similar vices. Could be a source of casual gossip or a witness to overheard conversations.

**Notes:** Expendable in the village's eyes. May or may not survive the flood or the unfolding crisis depending on circumstances.

### %WORKER_TWO% / %PIOTR%
**Type:** Farm laborer — younger, ambitious  
**Age in 1967:** ~20-25  
**Role:** A younger farmhand with dreams of owning his own land or leaving the village. Hard-working but resentful of the hierarchy. May have noticed %JUNIOR% with %BARBARA% or overheard conversations. Could be sympathetic to players or antagonistic depending on how they approach him. Less invested in the village's secrets than older workers—might be persuaded to talk.

**Notes:** A potential weak link in the village hierarchy. His ambitions make him either an ally or a problem.

### %COOK% / %ZOFIA%
**Type:** Farm cook — %WOJEWODA%'s household worker  
**Age in 1967:** ~45-55  
**Family:** Married to %FOREMAN% (farm overseer). Mother of %STASZEK% (age 5).  
**Role:** Cooks and manages meals for the farm workers and %WOJEWODA%'s household. Hears everything—workers talk freely around the cook. Knows about %JUNIOR% and %BARBARA%, about %WUJAS%'s drinking, about tensions in the family. Connected to %WOJEWODA%'s wife. May be protective of the household reputation or may be sympathetic to workers' plight depending on her nature.

**Notes:** A potential information goldmine if players can gain her trust or if she's upset about something happening on the farm.

- **Expandability:** If a secondary character becomes important to the story, promote them to a dedicated character file.
