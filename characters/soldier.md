# %SOLDIER%

**Type:** Named character — deceased settler / massacre participant

## Vital Statistics

- **Born:** 1910
- **Died:** 1954 (age 44)
- **Lived in:** [%CIOTKA%'s house](../places/new-village.md#ciotkas-house) (now [%CIOTKA%](ciotka.md)'s) — best plot in the village

## Character

Quiet neighbour, devoted father, kept to himself. Impossible to judge cleanly — the hands that held his children are the same hands that killed Lemkos in 1947. A monster who was also the best father on the street.

## Background

Member of military/security forces (likely KBW) deployed for Akcja Wisła. Present when the situation at [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) escalated — participated in the shooting of rebelling Lemko villagers. After the massacre, agreed with fellow soldiers to never speak of it; the event was erased from all records.

Left military service in 1948, ahead of the main colonist wave. Settled in [%NEW_VILLAGE%](../places/new-village.md) because he **knew the area from his service.** Arrived early, knew the terrain, claimed the best land. To other colonists, he looked lucky or connected. To the Lemko-descended siblings ([%CIOTKA%](ciotka.md) and family), the killer returned to the scene and took the choicest spoils. Built a life on the doorstep of mass murder — among the ruins, the overgrown cemetery, the crumbling *cerkiew*.

## Family

- **%SOLDIER%'s wife** (unnamed, born ~1915) — killed in the lynch (1954, age ~39)
- **[%JAGNA%](jagna.md)** — eldest daughter, his pride — killed in the lynch (beaten, raped)
- **[%GLUPEK%](glupek.md)** — infant son, mentally challenged — survived

## Death

Killed in 1954 during [the lynch](../story/ending-punishment-lynch.md) in [%NEW_VILLAGE%](../places/new-village.md). Intervened when [%WUJAS%](secondary-characters.md), [%WOJEWODA%](wojewoda.md), and [%BUTCHER%](butcher.md) attacked [%JAGNA%](jagna.md); killed trying to save her. [%BUTCHER%](butcher.md) then convinced the others it was too late for half measures — wife and infant [%GLUPEK%](glupek.md) had to die too. Wife was killed; [%GLUPEK%](glupek.md) survived. Bodies dumped into [the well](../story-facts/the-well.md) in [%OLD_VILLAGE%](../places/old-village.md) — the same well holding the Lemko massacre remains.
