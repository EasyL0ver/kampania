# Wanda Mazur

**Type:** Named character — minor NPC

## Vital Statistics

- **Age in 1967:** ~50s
- **Heritage:** Polish settler
- **Lives in:** Small house near the church
- **Settled:** After 1954
- **Husband:** Dead. Grain silo accident on the PGR farm — sent in to clear a blockage, suffocated under the grain. Happened ~1965. Michał Pytlak's ignorance of the danger, not malice.

## Character

Quiet, devout, harmless. Grieving widow. Her dead husband is still on the PGR ledger as an active worker — she doesn't know his death was covered up.

## Opinions

- **[ks. Władysław Pająk](priest.md)** — her main human contact. He's kind to her. She attends every service.
- **[Michał Pytlak](foreman.md)** — her husband's former boss. She bears him no grudge — accidents happen. She doesn't know his negligence killed Tadeusz, and she doesn't know the death was covered up.
