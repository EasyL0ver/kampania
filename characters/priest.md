**A protector.** %PRIEST% looks out for %GLUPEK% alongside %CIOTKA%. The church may be one of the few safe spaces for the boy — a place where even %BUTCHER% wouldn't touch him.

## In 1967

A man who hears fragments of the village's guilt every week in the confessional. Bound by the seal of confession — even if he suspects the worst, he cannot act on it. He protects %GLUPEK% out of compassion, duty, or perhaps because he senses the boy is connected to whatever darkness the village is hiding.

**%PRIEST% arrived in %NEW_VILLAGE% after the lynch.** He wasn't there when it happened. Everything he knows — or suspects — comes from confessions, observation, and the village's uneasy silences.
