# %GLUPEK%

**Type:** Named character — child of %SOLDIER%

## Vital Statistics

- **Born:** 1950
- **Age in 1967:** 17

## Role

Infant son of %SOLDIER%. Living in %NEW_VILLAGE%. Mentally challenged — **not congenital, caused by brain damage from strangling during the lynch.**

## What Happened

During the lynch (1954), after %JAGNA%, %SOLDIER%, and %SOLDIER%'s wife were killed, %BUTCHER% attempted to strangle %GLUPEK% to eliminate all witnesses. **%CIOTKA% intervened and saved his life** — but the oxygen deprivation had already caused permanent brain damage.

%GLUPEK% was **4 years old** at the time (born 1950). Too young to remember the event or his family.

## Fate

**Survived.** The only member of %SOLDIER%'s family left alive. Bears the physical evidence of the crime in his damaged mind — but has **no memory** of the event or his family.

**In 1967** (when the story takes place), %GLUPEK% is **17 years old** — a brain-damaged teenager, **physically massive and imposing**, living among the people who murdered his family. He doesn't understand what happened to him, but everyone who looks at him is reminded of what they did — or what they let happen.
