## The Well's Influence

The well amplifies %WUJAS%'s **guilt and self-destruction.** He was already the weakest vessel — 13 years of drinking to forget. The well makes the forgetting stop working.

- The drinking increases but stops numbing. He drinks the same amount and stays painfully, horribly lucid. The one thing that kept him functional — the ability to drown it — fails.
- Fragments leak out. Not confessions — worse. Half-sentences at the wrong moment. %JAGNA%'s name muttered in his sleep. *"She didn't deserve it"* to whoever's sitting next to him. He doesn't remember saying it. Others do.
- His sleep fills with the sound of the well — water dripping, echoing, rising. He wakes up hoarse, as if he's been shouting. The drinking crew notices. %JUNIOR% notices.
- The volatility spikes. He swings between maudlin weeping and sudden rage. Picks fights he'd normally avoid. Grabs people too hard. The violence under the surface — the same violence from 1954 — is closer to the skin.

The well doesn't need to break %WUJAS%. He was already broken. It just needs to make the cracks **audible**.
