# %MATRONA%

**Type:** Named character — sibling (sister)

## Vital Statistics

- **Born:** 1922
- **Age in 1967:** 45

## Role

One of four siblings — sister to %WOJEWODA%, %WUJAS%, and %CIOTKA%. Part of a family unit in %NEW_VILLAGE%.

## Heritage

Lemko descent — hidden. See %WOJEWODA% for details on the siblings' shared background.

## Relationships

- **%PAINTER%** — husband (married after the lynch)
- Siblings: %WOJEWODA%, %WUJAS%, %CIOTKA%

## Relationship to %SOLDIER%

**Deep, layered hatred.** On the surface — personal betrayal: %PAINTER% chose %JAGNA% over her. But beneath that: %SOLDIER% took the best land, his family thrived while hers hid their Lemko identity, and his teenage daughter humiliated her in front of the whole village. The affair was the last insult in a long chain.

## The Lynch — The True Architect

The village believes %BUTCHER% was the coldest head that night. **They're wrong. It was %MATRONA%.**

She didn't stumble to her brothers in tears after catching the lovers. She went to them **with intent**. She got them drinking. She fed the rage. She aimed them at %JAGNA% like a weapon.

When %SOLDIER% was dead and the drunk men were panicking, it was %MATRONA% — **sober, clear-eyed** — who said the wife and the baby had to die too. %BUTCHER% carried it out. But the logic was hers. The voice in the dark giving orders while three drunk men did the work.

She never touched anyone. Every hand that night was male. That's not an accident — it's her design.

## In 1967

Married to %PAINTER%. Respectable. The grieving sister-in-law, the good wife who forgave her fiancé's betrayal. A woman wronged who chose grace.

In reality: the architect of a massacre, hiding behind 13 years of performance. She's had longer to practice her story than anyone. She's the **hardest NPC to crack** — not because she won't talk, but because she'll talk plenty. She'll give the players a version of events that's almost true, with herself carefully edited out of every scene that matters.

**%PAINTER% is her insurance.** She's kept him broken, dependent, and terrified for 13 years. He knows the truth — he heard her voice that night — and she's made sure he'll never speak it. His guilt over the affair, his gratitude that she "forgave" him, his fear of what she's capable of — she wields all of it.

## Redemption

%MATRONA%'s redemption is the hardest in the story. She'd have to admit not just complicity but **authorship**. She designed the murder of a family, including a child, and spent 13 years letting others carry the guilt. Her path to redemption requires accepting that she is the worst person in this village — worse than the drunk men, worse than %BUTCHER% — because she was sober and she chose it.

## The Only Paths to Her

- **%PAINTER%** — if the players free him from her control. He heard her voice that night.
- **%BUTCHER%** — if he remembers the sober woman giving orders. The coldest head in the room may remember there was a colder one.
- **%CIOTKA%** — if still alive, she was there. She may know who really gave the order to kill the baby.
