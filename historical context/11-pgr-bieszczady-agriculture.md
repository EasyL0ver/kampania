# PGR (State Farms) in Bieszczady — 1960s

**Type:** Historical context — economic & administrative structure

## Overview

PGR (Państwowe Gospodarstwo Rolne — State Agricultural Farm) was the communist state's primary form of agricultural organization. PGRs were created from confiscated estates, former landed properties, and land seized after Operation Vistula.

In frontier areas like Bieszczady, PGRs were the **economic backbone** of resettled communities.

## Structure & Management

**National hierarchy:**
- Ministry of State Farms → Regional administration → Individual PGR directors (kierownik/dyrektor)

**In practice (especially remote areas):**
- Central oversight was minimal for isolated PGRs
- **Local manager (kierownik) had near-total autonomy** over:
  - Labor assignment and wages
  - Resource allocation
  - Punishment and discipline
  - Relations with the community
  - Local reporting to authorities

**Accountability was theoretical** — a strong manager in a remote PGR could operate almost independently.

## Farm Operations

**Typical Bieszczady PGR (~1960s):**
- Size: 200–600 hectares
- Staff: 15–40 workers depending on size
- Products: Grain, potatoes, some livestock (cattle, pigs)
- Organization: Shift-based labor, seasonal peaks, rigid hierarchy
- Conditions: Poor mechanization, harsh manual work, low wages

## Worker Status

Workers in a PGR were **state employees** but with minimal rights:
- No effective unions or worker protections
- Wages set by state formula (notoriously low)
- Living conditions often poor
- Subject to discipline by management
- Fear of being fired meant fear of destitution

%BARBARA%, despite being employed by the state, is effectively enslaved to %WOJEWODA% — she cannot leave, cannot complain, cannot organize. Her only leverage is silence (knowing the family's secrets) and her son (whose welfare depends on staying employed).

## The Silence

PGR villages like %NEW_VILLAGE% were **vertically organized control structures** disguised as economic units. The manager reported to the state, the state reported to the party, the party reported to Moscow. Independent thought or action was eliminated by dependency.
